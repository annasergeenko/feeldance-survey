Before you submit

Competition results in ballroom dancing are directly tied to the number of ballroom-specific hours per week. Time in other disciplines — ballet, gymnastics, soccer — does not substitute for this. The Scholarship Athletes track is reserved for students whose primary athletic commitment is ballroom dancing at Feel Dance Studio.

Competition results depend on ballroom-specific training hours — not on time spent in other sports or dance disciplines.
The Scholarship rate ($85/lesson) applies only when all track requirements are fully met every week — no exceptions.
